Lost and Found System.

Problem:
	There is need of a system for helping people locate their missing belongings 
	following a disaster. It'd be a distributed system. 
	
Universal Design:
	There are two methods of interaction
	1.	Touch screens: For user's familiar with technology
	2.	Digital Levers: Resembling mechanical levers, these physical elements will provide the familiarity of 
	old mechanical systems and the efficiency of digital devices.


Task Description:
There are four tasks that can be carried out by each user: 
1.	Check-in an item
2.	Checkout an item
3.	Lookup an item
4.	Add missing item
	

Assumptions:
The user is assumed to be familiar with conveyer belts and kiosks.
