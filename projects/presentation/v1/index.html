Project: Tab + Type
Team Size: 3
PM, Lead Engineer, Designer (Me)
Timeline: 4 weeks.
Business Goals: A highly requested feature by power users.
Problem Statement: Allow users to 'type' into text fields as you 'tab' between them.
Design Constraints: Static visuals would be arduous and would not solve the interaction problem.
Project: Tab + Type
Challenges: Mediation, Timeline and Bug resolution.
User Charateristics: They were heavy Excel users.
Design Challenges: Drag vs Edit mode, Overriding 'delete' key.
Affordances: Below is the evolution of affordances.

Always editable
Editable on click,
'enter' for new line
Editable on double click,
'enter' to commit
'alt + enter' for new line

Project: Simplified Sending
Team Size: 3
PM, Design Manager, 2 Designers, 2 Researchers, Prototyper + Motion Designer (Me)
Timeline: 4 months.
Business Goals: Decrease drop off of new users.
Research: Only about 50% of trial customers figured out how to send first document.
Design Pivot: The initial plan was to create in-product tips and video tutorials. Preliminary research revealed that similar previous attempts failed to improve user experience or conversion. The project was then upgraded to 'New Experience' for trial customers.
